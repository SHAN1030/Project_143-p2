AI PING PONG GAME



INSTRUCTIONS

1. First, keep your laptop or computer screen straight.

2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.

4. Now press the PLAY GAME button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

5. The red paddle on the left is your paddle.That will move as per the movementof your right wrist.
